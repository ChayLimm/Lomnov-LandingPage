Why Choose Lomnov?

Streamline your property management with our innovative Telegram-integrated platform designed for modern landlords.

Telegram Integration

Communicate with tenants directly through Telegram. Send reminders, announcements, and receive payments without switching apps.

Payment Tracking

Automatically track rent payments, generate invoices, and send payment reminders. Get real-time insights into your cash flow.

Bill Management

Easily manage utility bills, maintenance costs, and other expenses. Split bills between tenants automatically.
